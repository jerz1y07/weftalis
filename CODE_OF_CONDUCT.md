# Community Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want participation in Weftalis to be safe, respectful, and welcoming for people with different backgrounds and levels of experience.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Discuss ideas and code without attacking people.
- Give feedback that is specific and useful.
- Respect privacy, consent, authorship, and license requirements.
- Help beginners understand problems without ridicule.
- Accept reasonable maintainer decisions about safety and project scope.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention.
- Publishing another person's private information or credentials.
- Knowingly submitting malicious, deceptive, plagiarized, or unauthorized content.
- Repeated disruption after a maintainer asks for the behavior to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, reject, hide, or remove contributions and may limit participation when behavior threatens people, project safety, or constructive collaboration. Reports should not contain real secrets or unnecessary private information. Security-sensitive reports must follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
